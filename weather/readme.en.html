Setting

Weather service developed by Dev.QWeather, please register your development after the account, fill out the following developers information before use.

KEY

Your KEY is only saved in LocalStorage of your browser until you clear the browser cache. You do not need to set it again. We do not store any of your account information.

If your KEY has been reset, please remember to reset the key information so that the plug-in is not available.

How do I find developer information

Login your Dev.QWeather account, click on the top on the right side of the "Console", then choose "Project" on the left. Click the "Create Project" button, enter the project name, select the subscription method, personally recommend choosing a free subscription, but there is a limit of requests per day, then select "Web API", and finally create your project.

Once the project is created, click "View" under the table "KEY" on the "Project" page to see the KEY you need.
